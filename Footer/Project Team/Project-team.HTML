ABOUT PROJECT

Our major project revolves around developing a comprehensive system for the Association of Caramoan Owners of Tourist Accommodation (ACOTA). With a team member actively involved in managing a homestay, we leverage firsthand insights to create a user-friendly web platform. Our objective is to streamline processes for ACOTA members, offering an intuitive solution to manage tourist accommodations efficiently. By combining our web development skills with a commitment to practicality, we aim to enhance the experience for both accommodation owners and tourists alike. Through this project, we demonstrate the tangible impact of technology in addressing the real needs of communities, showcasing the practicality and relevance of our web development expertise.
