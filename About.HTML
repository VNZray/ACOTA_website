ABOUT WEBSITE

Welcome to our website, your portal to the captivating destination of Caramoan. Our primary goal is to entice tourists and travelers to explore the beauty of Caramoan, a tropical paradise in the Philippines. Simultaneously, we're committed to supporting the local economy by connecting hotel and homestay owners with potential customers, helping their businesses thrive. Caramoan offers an array of accommodations, from cozy homestays to luxurious hotels, all of which can be conveniently found and booked through our platform. By choosing to stay in these local establishments, you not only enjoy an authentic experience but also contribute to the growth and prosperity of the local community. Come, discover Caramoan, and make a meaningful impact on both your journey and the local businesses that welcome you.

OUR MISSION AND VISION

MISSION

As an Association of Accommodation Owners, We Commit To:
  1. Continuously strengthen our organization.
  2. Provide a venue for consultations among members towards identifying the issues and concerns they encounter;
  3. Recommend policies and programs toward the attainment of sustainable tourism;
  4. Build partnership and linkages with other groups and;
  5. Be an active partner of the LGU in tourism development.

VISION

We want to see the Association of Caramoan Owners of Tourist Accommodations to become Strong and Responsible Organization pushing for Sustainable Development of Caramoan.
