ISLE DE BELLA BEACH HOTEL


Isle De Bella Beach Hotel is located in Sohoton Paniman, Caramoan Camarines Sur (Bicol Region) features seven well-appointed rooms, providing a welcoming retreat for guests seeking comfort and convenience in this stunning locale. In addition to the cozy accommodations, the homestay provides complimentary Wi-Fi access, ensuring that guests stay connected and informed throughout their stay. To enhance the experience, a karaoke setup is also available, adding a fun and lively dimension to their visit. With the perfect blend of comfort, modern amenities, and entertainment, Kim Angela Homestay is an ideal choice for those looking to make the most of their time in this beautiful area.
